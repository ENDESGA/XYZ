### <span style="color: #888;">MATH::</span>HANGLE
In 2D space you have `angle` which represents a rotation around the XY origin.
Angles can be measured in `radians` or `degrees`: 1 full rotation = 360 degrees = 6.28319~ radians.

In ***3D*** space you have `hangle` *(hyper-angle)* which represents a rotation around the XYZ origin.
Hangles can be measured in `steradians` or `desolids`: 1 full rotation = 720 desolids = 12.56637~ steradians.
### for all Structures, hangles are measured in ***steradians*** by default.
So all functions that have `hangle` inputs are measured in `steradians`.
